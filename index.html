Questions, answered

What does Single File do?

I file your intakes, assessments, progress notes, and treatment plans into the correct charts in your Office Ally (EHR 24/7) system, and sign them off on the schedule you choose.

Is Single File HIPAA-compliant?

Yes. Before any patient information is handled, we sign a HIPAA Business Associate Agreement. The service is US-based and run by one person with no subcontractors, documents move only through a BAA-covered Google Workspace system, and patient information is never sent to any third-party AI or outside processing service.

How do I send my documents?

Whichever way is easiest: email them to your practice's secure address, or drop them into a shared folder. From there, I file at the cadence you prefer.

Which EHR does Single File support?

Single File specializes in Office Ally (EHR 24/7); that deep focus is why the filing lands right every time. Using a different EHR, like SimplePractice, TherapyNotes, or TheraNest? Reach out anyway: I'm open to bringing the same service to other systems where the workflow fits.

Do you keep copies of our patients' records?

No. The permanent record lives in your own Office Ally chart. My working copies are transient and are cleared after upload; system backups deliberately exclude all patient documents, and I never sell or reuse patient data.

What does it cost?

A one-time setup fee, then a modest per-document rate. You pay only for what I actually file.
